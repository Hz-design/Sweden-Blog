Going to the Ikea in Birsta

Third day

23.1.14

Visiting the fourth Ikea ever openend and Stornäsets naturreservat.

07:00

Heerlijk gedoucht in de ochtend en vervolgens wezen ontbijten.

09:30

Richting de Ikea, eerste keer met de bus! Met de applicatie Din Tur kan je kaartjes kopen voor de bus, een enkeltje kost 22.00 SEK voor 20 - 25 år en 15.99 SEK voor 0 - 19 år, ik maakte zoals gewoonlijk misbruik van het systeem 🤣.

Samen met Eelco gingen we naar de bus we namen bus 2(Stadstrafik 2) towards Ljustadalen via Östra Birsta. De rit duurde circa 20 minuten en kwamen naast Ikea eruit. In the bus we met with Anna, Alessio and Alvaro.

14:06

After arriving we went to the shoppingmall and explored the area, flowershops, lots of apoteks, phone stores and more. I bought some cosmetics and also wanted to buy soles with spikes but those were 229SEK so too expensive at the time. And Wikki joined the party!

15:22

In the Ikea I got my bedsheets, cooking utensils and some useful things. After shopping we went to a second hand store and bought a poster for 10SEK and I lost it :.) On the way to the car Jonas W. has a car, we came across Claudia and Óscar, they arrived a little bit later. Little chit chat and we went on, me and Eelco joined Jonas W. for a walk at Stornäsets naturreservat! It is a very beautiful places, surrounded by ice and silence. We walked through a frozen swamp and the snow was very thick as you can see on the pictures.

15:38

Later on we came across a firepit, it was still on so we added some wood to enjoy the fire. When the sun was setting and when it became dark we decided to go back home. The view was great! Beautiful view of the skyline of Sundsvall.

18:14

After arriving home we decided to meet up later in the evening! But first food! I started with a salad and soup it was bell pepper soup. After that on the menu was potatoes with lightly breaded snitzel with bernaise sauce. And I really liked the table decoration it looks like local plants, some rosemary and some local plants, smelt very nice.

20:45

Tonight Mia suggested to go to a bar called O'Learys, they have darts, pooling, and some arcade games! Me and Elco met up and were walking to the sportsbar because it was very close to us we took a little detour. To our suprise it had 2 floors and it was really cozy and nice inside! But we where here for a reason! Drinking, we teached the international people how to play bussen, the Spanish knew a variation till the pyramid, it was a fun night where we get to know everyone better! A lot of people actually came so, Wikki, Mia, Anna, Alessio, Alvaro, Óscar, Claudia, Annabel, Loreto, Niclas, Marie, Vincent, Timo and Jonas. Jonas W. and Dhana also joined later. When I was talking with Jonas W. and Dhana, we had a weird encouter with some swedish high school girls, they were daring us who could hit harder, but in the end nothing extraordinary happend. On the way back me and Eelco saw someone puking and a lot of puke.
