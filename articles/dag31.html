Day 31

23.2.11

08:12

I woke up at 07:45 and still had to pack my backpack, in a hurry I finished packing and making myself ready to leave. I somehow managed to be ready at 08:12 and we departed to the ICA to buy some foood and booze!

09:44

After finishing groceries we didn't managed to buy some booze. Because System Bolaget opens later on the day.. at 10:00 and we couldn't afford to wait for it and than leave. So our journey went on. We made a little break at a gasstation near the High Coast Bridge ,we went to enjoy the view looked inside the souvenir shop and had a pee break.

10:53

We have arrived at National Park Skulenskogen West Entrance. We are ready to go! 2,7 km hike to the Tärnättvattnen Cabin.

12:01

The hike ended up being 1 hour and 8 minutes long not to bad! We were so glad that we had arrived because it is very exhausting to hike with 20kg backpacks. As we went inside we were amazed by the Cabin. It was super clean, smelled nice, felt very cozy and it was freezing cold inside. There were also very funny diaries inside the cabin!

17:17

On our first day we didn't do much, we only stayed around the Cabin! We also met some people, Jorum, 4 ladies working as taxlawyers and an old couple. They were all enjoying a lunch at the campfire near the cabin! Very kind people. We looked around the lake and tried to fly the drone! Eventually we stayed inside because we were tired and cold. Before heading out to make some hotdogs we played some games if I remembered correctly and chatted.

22:00

After eating the hotdogs we went inside to drink what we had and played games like iPuke very fun game haha ;). Everyone was very tired so we didn't watched a horror movie or something like that, we went sleeping around 23:00! Me and Eelco together, Alessio and Álvaro and Jonas W. in the main living room. He closed the door 1 hour after I was sleeping because of the snoring xD.
